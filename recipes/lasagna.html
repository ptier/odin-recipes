Lasagna Recipe

this is a beautiful pic of lasagna

Have you ever had lasagna? This is the most epic lasagna you will ever make - even better than grandma's!!!!

Ingredients

  • Buttah
  • Pasta Noodles
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Ground Beef

Steps

Follow these steps in ORDER or else you'll murder your pasta!

  1. Lay down the first layer of pasta noodles
  2. Lay down a layer of meat
  3. Lay down a layer of buttah
  4. Lay down a layer of cheese
  5. Lay down a layer of meat sauce
  6. Repeat the steps until it's as thick as you want
  7. Bake the thing until it's done.
